Services

Future Tech Partners is a boutique advisory focused on fusion energy and the enabling technologies around it (HTS magnets, cryogenics, lasers, advanced materials, power systems).

We work with fusion companies, investors, suppliers, utilities, hyperscalers, and industrial offtakers who are deciding when and how to participate in fusion, while continuing to support select non-fusion deep-tech startups and investors where our experience is relevant.

As part of our fusion services, we publish The Fusion Conclusion: a free weekly newsletter for the "fusion curious", which features essays on the state and trajectory of the fusion industry, technical deep dives, and fortnightly industry updates.

Fusion Strategy & Industry Forecasting

For companies and institutions that need a clear view of where fusion is actually going and what it means for them.

We help you:

  • understand realistic fusion timelines and technology scenarios;
  • map how fusion could affect your business model, assets, and competitive position;
  • identify when to move (and when to wait) on partnerships, PPAs, pilots, or investments; and
  • communicate fusion opportunities and risks in plain language for boards and decision makers.

Typical clients: Utilities, hyperscalers and data center operators, industrial heat users, OEMs/suppliers, corporate strategy teams.

Fusion Technical & Commercial Due Diligence

For investors and strategic buyers evaluating fusion and fusion-adjacent companies.

We provide science-first diligence with a focus on commercial reality, assessing:

  • technical feasibility from first principles (plasma physics, magnets, power systems, cryogenics);
  • strengths and weaknesses of the IP and know-how;
  • capital intensity, pathway to commercialization, and key execution risks; and
  • founding and technical team ability to deliver against milestones.

We also perform deep-tech diligence outside of fusion (advanced materials, lasers, isotopes, industrial processes) where our background is relevant.

Typical clients: VC and growth funds, infrastructure and sovereign investors, corporate venture arms, family offices.

Fusion Policy & Government Interface

For industry and government leaders seeking to turn fusion enthusiasm into concrete programs and outcomes.

We translate between the languages of science, business, and policy to:

  • connect national objectives with realistic fusion technology roadmaps;
  • structure effective public-private partnerships and funding mechanisms;
  • review and stress-test fusion-related policy proposals; and
  • help companies position themselves for government partnerships, grants, and loan programs.

Typical clients: Government agencies, national labs, economic development groups, fusion companies engaging with public programs.

Fractional CTO & Venture Partner

For inventors, scientists, and engineers ready to commercialize fusion or other game-changing technologies.

We offer startup technical leadership and direction in:

  • technology and product roadmapping;
  • risk identification and mitigation;
  • intellectual property strategy;
  • systems engineering and management setup; and
  • team building, organizational design, and technical hiring.

Typical clients: Early-stage fusion and deep-tech startups, especially where the founding team wants an experienced technical co-pilot without adding a full-time executive.

If you’re unsure which of these fits your situation, reach out and we can decide together whether and how we can help in fusion or beyond.
